What is MCAP

The Mzansi Climate Action Platform is built to enable young people aged 13-20 from the rural areas & townships of South Africa to get involved in Climate Action Initiatives.

Join Us

Our Climate Action Initiatives

These are our different Climate Action Initiatives that young people can get involved in.

Earth Day

Invest in our planet & join the world's largest Environmental Movement.

Plant A Tree

We encourage young people to celebrate Arbor Day & the whole of Arbor Month.

Urban Farming

We enable young people to establish urban food gardens & social farms to alleviate poverty.

Wastepreneurs

Using MCAP, young people can be able to find & support their local Wastepreneurs.

Waste Recycling

Our AR challenges incentivise young people to recycle their waste and earn points.

Public Cleaning

Our platform mobilizes young people to conduct Mass Public Cleaning campaigns.
